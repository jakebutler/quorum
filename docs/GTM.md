# Quorum GTM Plan

## Positioning

Quorum is the fastest way to get expert pass/fail feedback on HTML artifacts and design options. The wedge is simple: most teams do not need a heavyweight research platform to start collecting eval data. They need a URL, a pass/fail button, notes, ranking, and CSV.

## ICP

- AI product teams building first eval sets from expert review.
- Design and growth teams comparing landing-page concepts.
- Agencies that need structured client feedback without exposing internal tooling.

## Launch Offer

Install the template, add review artifacts, deploy to Cloudflare Pages, and download CSV from the hosted dashboard. No accounts for reviewers. No complex setup.

## Channels

- Founder/dev demo post: short video showing `npx quorum init`, dropping files into `content/reviews`, deployment, and dashboard CSV download.
- GitHub README: optimized for agents and human developers.
- Corvo Labs landing page at `quorum.corvolabs.com`.
- Direct outreach to teams already doing ad hoc expert reviews in spreadsheets.
- Community posts in AI engineering, product ops, and design engineering groups.

## Proof Points

- Static frontend deploys anywhere.
- Hosted backend keeps reviewers accountless.
- CSV export works for eval pipelines and design decision records.
- MIT template reduces adoption friction.

## Launch Checklist

- Public `corvolabs/quorum` repo with MIT license and complete README.
- `quorum.corvolabs.com` Worker deployed with provisioning, API, dashboard, export, and landing page.
- Demo project deployed to Cloudflare Pages.
- 90-second demo video.
- Example CSV and sample review package in the repo.
- First 10 design partners invited with concierge setup.

## Metrics

- Template clones.
- Provisioned projects.
- Review sessions started and completed.
- CSV exports.
- First-week activated teams: at least one completed session and one export.
